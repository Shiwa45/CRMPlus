# 🚀 Ultimate CRM Master Blueprint (India-Focused)

This document serves as the exhaustive development roadmap for transitioning the application into a top-tier, highly competitive CRM in the Indian market.

## Phase 1: Core Sales & Contact Management (The Foundation)

### 1. 📇 Contact & Lead Management
- [ ] **Lead Capture**
  - [ ] Web forms / landing page integration
  - [ ] WhatsApp lead capture
  - [ ] Social media lead capture (Meta, LinkedIn, YouTube)
  - [ ] IndiaMart, JustDial, TradeIndia, Sulekha integration
  - [ ] Google Ads / Meta Ads lead sync
  - [ ] Bulk import (Excel/CSV)
- [ ] **Contact Management**
  - [ ] 360° contact profile (Multiple phone numbers/emails)
  - [ ] Company & individual mapping
  - [ ] Document storage against contacts (Aadhaar, PAN, GST certificates)
  - [ ] Duplicate detection & merge
- [ ] **Lead Management**
  - [ ] Lead source tracking & attribution
  - [ ] Lead distribution (round-robin, territory, skill-based)
  - [ ] Lead conversion to opportunity/deal
  - [ ] Junk/dead lead management

### 2. 💼 Sales Management
- [ ] **Pipeline Management**
  - [ ] Visual drag-and-drop pipeline (Kanban)
  - [ ] Multiple pipelines for different products/services
  - [ ] Stage-wise probability & Weighted pipeline value
- [ ] **Opportunity/Deal Management**
  - [ ] Deal value & expected close date
  - [ ] Deal splitting (co-selling)
  - [ ] Deal notes, activities, timeline & Document attachments
- [ ] **Reporting & Dashboards (Basic)**
  - [ ] Real-time interactive dashboards
  - [ ] Lead reports (source, status, conversion)
  - [ ] Sales reports (pipeline, revenue, target vs. achievement)

## Phase 2: Quotes, Invoicing, and Indian Market Essentials

### 3. 💸 Quotes, Invoicing & Payments
- [ ] **Quotation & Proposal Management**
  - [ ] GST-compliant quotation (CGST, SGST, IGST, Cess)
  - [ ] Multi-line item quotes & Template generation
  - [ ] PDF generation & sharing via WhatsApp/Email
  - [ ] Quote-to-invoice conversion
- [ ] **Invoice & Billing**
  - [ ] GST-compliant invoicing & E-invoicing (GST portal standards)
  - [ ] Credit note / debit note & Recurring invoicing
  - [ ] E-way bill generation
- [ ] **Payment Tracking**
  - [ ] Payment collection tracking & Partial payments
  - [ ] UPI payment link generation
  - [ ] Razorpay / PayU / Cashfree integration

### 4. 📞 Communication & Engagement (Omnichannel)
- [ ] **WhatsApp Integration ⭐ (Critical)**
  - [ ] WhatsApp Business API integration
  - [ ] Two-way WhatsApp communication in CRM
  - [ ] Template message management & Bulk messaging
- [ ] **Telephony / Cloud Calling**
  - [ ] Built-in cloud telephony & Click-to-call
  - [ ] Call recording & storage (Exotel / Knowlarity integration)
  - [ ] Call logs auto-capture & Missed call tracking
- [ ] **Email & SMS Integration**
  - [ ] Email tracking (open, click, reply) & Bulk campaigns
  - [ ] DLT-registered Promotional/Transactional SMS
